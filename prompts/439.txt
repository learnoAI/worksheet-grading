```json

    {
      "question_number": 1,
      "question": "Q1. 9 , 49",
```

- `"question_number"`: **INTEGER**. Extract only the numeric value (e.g., `1`, `2`, `30`). Do NOT include the "Q" prefix here.

3. **Handwriting & Digit Recognition Guidelines:**
   - Look extremely closely at digit formation to avoid misclassifications.
   - Carefully distinguish between **1** and **2** (e.g., ensure you do not read "19" as "29").
   - Distinguish **9** vs **8**, **6** vs **0**, and **1** vs **7** vs **4**.

4. **Processing Order:**
   - The worksheet features two vertical columns per page.
   - Read and track the question numbers carefully to ensure all 40 questions are captured.
   - Sort the final output array by `question_number` in ascending order (1 to 40).
