1.  **`question_number` (Integer):**

- **Analyze Ambiguous Digits Carefully:** Pay close attention to poorly formed numbers based on stroke context.
- **Specific Confusions to Avoid:**
    - **1 vs. 4:** Look for faint cross-strokes or vertical drops making a 1 actually a 4 (e.g., ensure you don't misread "44" as "14").

    - **3 vs. 6 vs. 0:** Check loop closures (e.g., don't misread "460" as "430").
    - **9 vs. 8** and **1 vs. 7**.
- If a number is messy, select the most likely digit based on visual stroke evidence rather than guessing a mathematically correct answer (extract what is *written*, right or wrong).

**PROCESSING ORDER:**
- Read column by column or track the sequential "Q" labels to ensure correct ordering.
- Sort the final JSON output by `question_number` in ascending order (1 through 40).
